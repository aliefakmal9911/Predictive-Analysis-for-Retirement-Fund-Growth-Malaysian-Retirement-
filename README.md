# Predictive-Analysis-for-Retirement-Fund-Growth-Malaysian-Retirement-
To assist the Employees Provident Fund (EPF) organization and its members in estimating the required retirement funds by 2030. 

• Developed a Predictive Analysis Model for Retirement Sufficiency Prediction by age 60 which calculate if your EPF money is enough to retire comfortably by calculating current EPF balance, annual EPF contribution and annual dividend rate.
• Developed a Predictive Analysis Model for 2030 Retirement Fund Growth which calculate the required fund needed to retire on the year 2030. From the project, estimated fund needed to retire in 2030 is RM465,680.40
